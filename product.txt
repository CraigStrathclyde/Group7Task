PRODUCT SELECTION AREA:
- Products are displayed with an image, name, price, and "Add to Basket" button.
  - Each product card includes:
    - Product Image: Clickable, opens a modal with more details.
    - Product Name: Descriptive and clickable, leading to the product detail page.
    - Price: Displayed clearly next to the product name.
    - Add to Basket Button: Adds the item to the shopping basket.
